About Sub-Clan Surveys
What is a Sub-Clan Survey?
A questionnaire specifically designed for collecting data from a particular sub-clan (Kanumbha) within the Bwamba Kingdom
Target Audience
Members and residents of the specific sub-clan where data collection is focused
Data Collection
Collects detailed information specific to the sub-clan's demographics, culture, and needs
Hierarchical Structure
Part of the broader clan data collection system, providing granular insights
Automatic Question Copy

This sub-clan survey will automatically copy all questions from the parent clan survey.

This ensures consistency in data collection across the clan hierarchy while allowing sub-clan specific responses.

  • All question types will be preserved (text, multiple choice, etc.)
  • Question order and requirements will be maintained
  • You can edit questions after creation if needed
